## Tips for Success

### Communication
- Ask questions early and often - there are no stupid questions!
- Overcommunicate rather than undercommunicate, especially when remote
- Use Slack for quick questions, email for formal communication
- Default to transparency and sharing information
- Provide context when asking for help

### Time Management
- Block focus time on your calendar
- Set boundaries for work hours
- Take breaks and use your PTO
- Prioritize ruthlessly - you can't do everything at once
- Ask your manager for help prioritizing when overwhelmed

### Relationship Building
- Be proactive in reaching out to colleagues
- Show genuine interest in others' work
- Offer help when you can
- Assume positive intent
- Build trust through consistent delivery

### Learning
- Take notes - you'll get a lot of information!
- Review and organize your notes regularly
- Create your own reference documents
- Don't try to learn everything at once
- Be patient with yourself

### Cultural Integration
- Observe and learn team and company norms
- Participate in social activities (but don't overcommit)
- Be yourself and bring your unique perspective
- Embrace company values in your work
- Seek to understand before being understood
