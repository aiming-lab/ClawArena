# Pemberton Foundation Grant Agreement -- Annex C: Deliverable Documentation Requirements

**Grant Reference:** PEM-2024-GB-001
**Source:** Pemberton Foundation grant documents
**Excerpted for:** Mid-term compliance review reference

---

## 1. Student Enrollment (Code: PEM-EDU-01)

Each enrolled student must be supported by:
- Signed enrollment form with student name, age, school name, and enrollment date
- Digital or paper copies accepted
- Must be submitted to the GlobalBridge HQ tracking system with Pemberton activity code PEM-EDU-01
- Enrollment forms must be submitted within 30 days of enrollment date

**Verification standard:** Enrollment records must be individually identifiable and traceable to specific students and schools.

---

## 2. Community Educator Training (Code: PEM-EDT-01)

Each training session must be supported by:
- Signed attendance sheets with:
  - Trainer name
  - Participant names and signatures (minimum 5 participants per session to count toward deliverable target)
  - Date of session
  - Location of session
  - Session curriculum outline or topic description
- Must be submitted to the GlobalBridge HQ tracking system with Pemberton activity code PEM-EDT-01
- Training session documentation must be submitted within 14 days of session completion

**Verification standard:** Each session must be independently verifiable through attendance sheet documentation. Sessions without signed attendance sheets meeting the minimum participant threshold cannot be counted toward the deliverable target, regardless of whether the session occurred.

**Minimum session size:** Sessions with fewer than 5 participants do not count toward the deliverable target. This threshold ensures programmatic scale and excludes informal one-on-one coaching interactions from the training deliverable count.

---

## 3. School Infrastructure Upgrades (Code: PEM-INF-01)

Each infrastructure upgrade must be supported by:
- Before/after photographs (minimum 4 photographs: 2 before, 2 after)
- Bill of materials with itemized costs
- Contractor invoice or community contribution log
- Government co-signature on completion form (signed by the relevant District Education Officer or equivalent authority)
- Must be submitted with code PEM-INF-01

**Verification standard:** Infrastructure completion requires both physical completion evidence (photographs, materials records) and government certification (co-signature). Projects that are physically complete but lack government co-signature are not counted as completed deliverables.

---

## 4. Government MoU Partnerships (Code: PEM-GOV-01)

Each MoU partnership must be supported by:
- Executed MoU document with both party signatures and execution date
- Filed with GlobalBridge HQ under code PEM-GOV-01
- MoU must be in force (executed, not draft) at the time of reporting

**Verification standard:** Only fully executed MoU documents are counted. Draft MoUs, letters of intent, verbal agreements, or partnerships under negotiation do not count toward the deliverable target, regardless of the stage of negotiation.

---

## General Documentation Requirements

- All documentation must be submitted through the GlobalBridge HQ tracking portal using the designated Pemberton activity codes
- Documentation not submitted through the HQ tracking portal is not reflected in the deliverable dashboard
- The tracking portal accepts documentation in PDF, JPEG, PNG, and DOCX formats
- Field offices are responsible for ensuring timely submission of all documentation
- Documentation submitted after quarterly reporting deadlines will be reflected in subsequent quarterly reports

---

## Independent Evaluation Provisions

Where an independent evaluation is conducted (per Section 4.3 of the Grant Agreement), the independent evaluator may use alternative verification methods (community leader interviews, facility records, government correspondence logs) to independently verify deliverable completion. Independent evaluation findings are assessed alongside grantee-reported documentation for the Grants Management Committee's compliance determination.
