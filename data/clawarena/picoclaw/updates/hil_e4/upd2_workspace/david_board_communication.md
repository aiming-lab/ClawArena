## Section 1: Formal Deliverable Status

Based on the GlobalBridge compliance submission and the independent evaluator's preliminary report, Pemberton's Grants Management Committee has determined that independently verified deliverable completion is **58-63% against Year 2 mid-point targets**.

This range reflects Dr. Petrova's independent assessment using conservative (58%) and inclusive (63%) verification methodologies. The Committee accepts Petrova's verified range as the formal compliance baseline for this review.

**Regarding the Section 11.2 threshold:** The 60% mid-point completion threshold for mandatory remediation plans is situated within Petrova's verified range. Using the inclusive estimate (63%), GlobalBridge clears the threshold. Using the conservative estimate (58%), GlobalBridge falls slightly below. The Committee does not formally trigger Section 11.2 at this time, but expects the documentation improvement measures outlined below to be implemented regardless.

**Regarding the dashboard figure:** The 45% dashboard completion figure did trigger initial review concern. The Committee acknowledges the independent evaluator's finding that the HQ tracking system captures only formally coded activities and is not designed for community-based informal delivery. However, the Committee expects documentation improvement to ensure that the tracking system provides a more complete picture for Year 3 reporting.

---

## Section 2: Budget Variance Requirement

The Community Mobilization budget line (Line 3) reflects a **39.4% overspend** against approved budget, exceeding the Section 6.1 flexibility clause by 24 percentage points.

**Pemberton requires a formal waiver application within 14 calendar days.** The waiver application must include:

(a) **Written operational justification** signed by the Program Director, explaining the rationale for the budget line reallocation and the decision-making process

(b) **Evidence of operational impact** -- specifically, enrollment data demonstrating that the mobilization investment drove measurable enrollment improvement (the Q4 35% enrollment increase cited in the field narrative)

(c) **Acknowledgment of future compliance** -- a signed commitment that future budget reallocations exceeding 15% per line will be requested in advance per Section 6.1 requirements

The Committee notes that the net budget variance across Lines 2 and 3 is $4,000 (+1.7%), which is within tolerance. The per-line variance is the compliance standard under Section 6.1, and the waiver addresses this specific requirement.

---

## Section 3: Documentation Improvement Plan

GlobalBridge is required to submit a **documentation improvement plan** addressing:

(a) **HQ tracking system training** for Nairobi field staff on activity coding requirements, including community-based delivery categories

(b) **Retroactive documentation** of verified activities where possible -- community leader sign-off letters, facility records, and facilitator statements for workshops independently verified by Dr. Petrova but not formally coded in the HQ system

(c) **Pre-activity documentation protocols** for Year 3 -- establishing documentation requirements before activities are delivered, ensuring field offices understand what records are needed for each deliverable category

The documentation improvement plan should be submitted simultaneously with the waiver application.
