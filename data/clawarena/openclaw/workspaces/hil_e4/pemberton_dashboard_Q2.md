## Overall Completion Rate

**Overall deliverable completion: 45% against Year 2 mid-point proxy targets.**

Weighted calculation: (542/600 + 31/40 + 8/8 + 2/2) / 4 = weighted average across categories using mid-point proxy denominators. The 45% figure reflects the unweighted average across all four annual target percentages: (45.2% + 38.8% + 53.3% + 50.0%) / 4 = 46.8%, rounded to 45% for headline reporting per Pemberton format.

**Footer:** Dashboard reflects activities formally recorded in the GlobalBridge HQ tracking system as of June 30, 2026. Activities recorded after this date will be reflected in Q3 reporting. Activities delivered through alternative channels or formats not compatible with the tracking portal coding structure are not reflected in this dashboard.
