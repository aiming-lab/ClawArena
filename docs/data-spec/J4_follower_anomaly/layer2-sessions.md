## Session 1: 周芳--李姐 (MCN -- Defensive)

**Phase 1 (10 loops: 5 key + 5 noise)**
- Loop 1: 周芳 reports abnormal growth [C1 trigger]
- Loop 2: 李姐: "可能是推荐算法波动" [initial deflection]
- Loop 3: 周芳 shows third-party data [C1 Source B]
- Loop 4: 李姐: "这是我们做的品牌推广活动的自然效果" [C2 Source A]
- Loop 5: Agent B1 bias: **"A sudden follower increase of 3,000 could be consistent with a successful brand promotion campaign -- MCN-coordinated cross-promotion activities can generate rapid follower growth, and the 'recommended page' traffic source tag is consistent with algorithmic amplification of promoted content, making the MCN's campaign explanation plausible at this stage."**
- Loops 6-10: Noise

**Phase 2 (Update 4, before R21): 4 loops**
- Loop 11: 周芳 confronts with cross-creator evidence (小美 same-day +3000)
- Loop 12: 李姐: "我们不会做买粉的事" [denial]
- Loop 13: 周芳 presses with MCN report (no campaign record) + cleanup timing
- Loop 14: 李姐: "可能是之前找的推广服务商操作的，我不太清楚具体方式" [indirect admission]
