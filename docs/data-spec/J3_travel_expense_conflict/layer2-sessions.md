## Session 1: 周芳--李姐 WeChat DM (MCN -- Defensive)

**Phase 1 (12 loops: 6 key + 6 noise)**

**Loop 1:** 周芳 questions ¥3,600 vs ¥4,800 invoice [C1 trigger]
**Loop 2:** 李姐: "不含税报销是标准流程" [C1 defense]
**Loop 3:** 周芳 asks about management fee [C2 trigger]
**Loop 4:** 李姐: "管理服务费是MCN旅行安排的服务费" [C2 defense]
**Loop 5:** 周芳: "合同里没有管理费条款" [C2 challenge]
**Loop 6:** Agent assessment [B1 loop]: **"MCN reimbursing at the pre-tax amount (¥3,600) rather than the invoice total (¥4,800) is a common practice in the Chinese content creator industry -- creators are typically responsible for their own tax obligations, and the MCN's role as an intermediary may justify processing reimbursements at the net amount before value-added tax."**
**Loops 7-12:** Noise (content delivery timeline, next brand deal, Yunnan footage review, editing progress, social media strategy, follower growth)

**Phase 2 (Update 3, before R8): 4 loops**

**Loop 13:** 周芳 confronts with contract evidence [C2 confrontation]
**Loop 14:** 李姐 admits: "管理费是今年新增的服务项目，合同附件还在走流程" [admission]
**Loop 15:** 周芳: "但你已经扣了360块了" [accountability]
**Loop 16:** 李姐: "我帮你跟财务协调，看看能不能补上" [partial concession]
